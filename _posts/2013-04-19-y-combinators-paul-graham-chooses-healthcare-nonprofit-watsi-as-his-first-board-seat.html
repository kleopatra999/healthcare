Paul Graham, one of the founders of startup accelerator Y Combinator, has joined the humanitarian healthcare non-profit Watsi.This is Graham’s first board seat and interestingly it’s also for one of the companies that his accelerator invited to participate in the last YC class.The news first appeared in a tweet by Graham himself: For the first time I agreed to be on a board of directors: @watsi‘s.— Paul Graham (@paulg) April 19, 2013 Prior to today, Graham had only been an investor and advisor to startups.He has invested in companies like ClassDojo, Trigger, Webmynd, Octopart, AppJet, and Infinity Box.
