Heading to the emergency room?You may wait a while before a doctor or other treating professional sees you — and the hospital nearest to you might not be the one that sees you the fastest.Use this tool to look up average ER wait times, as reported by hospitals to the federal government, as well as the time it takes to get there in current traffic, as reported by Google.Find out more » This app displays only average wait times.For a current wait time, call the hospital.
