OKLAHOMA CITY – An Oklahoma City surgery center is offering a new kind of price transparency, posting guaranteed all-inclusive surgery prices online.The move is revolutionizing medical billing in Oklahoma and around the world.Dr.Keith Smith and Dr.Steven Lantier launched Surgery Center of Oklahoma 15 years ago, founded on the simple principle of price honesty.
